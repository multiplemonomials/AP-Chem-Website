Thursday 9/11/14 Discuss Test #1

Are the fish safe to eat?
  We will investigate the Fukushima Power Plant Meltdown.  This will involve the researching the following items.
1.  How a nuclear power plant works
2.  What is a meltdown?
3.  How is Fukushima releasing radioactive materials into the enviroment,
4.  Where is radioactive material from Fukushima being found
5.  How are the fish involved?
6.  Where have we found radioactive fish?
7.  What is a dangerous level or radiation to humans?

We will produce an informative flyer (pamphlet) that will give facts and help consumers decide if our seafood is safe to eat. 

1.  It will include real facts from cited sources.
2.  It will contain some graphs or diagrams that give proof to pollution from the power plant.
3.  It will include relevant pictures that catch the readers attention
4.  It will provide a summary of what happened at Fukushima, including the contaminated water supply.

***please pay attention to dates of written articles and sources.  Many items are newer and have updated from the original situation**
